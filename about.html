Matthew Goldstein is the author of The Dark Mind Trilogy. The third book, Voices in the Night, was released in August 2015. An epic fantasy novel is in the works. He has been writing novels since he was a teenager, winning first place in his college's short story competition and runner-up in a Writer's Digest Short Story Competition. He worked for seven years as a Game Designer at 1st Playable Productions where his job involved writing scripts for video games. He received his degree from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. In his spare time, he loves to play drums and read to his daughter. He lives in Pennsylvania. He can be contacted at matthewgoldsteinbooks@gmail.com
